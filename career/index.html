Middle Web designer

Apply

If you are passionate about web design and ready to become a member of our professional team join our awesome marketing department! We are an open-minded and progressive company that enjoys exciting challenges and innovation. You will have a chance to grow your technical and soft skills and we will invest into your professional training, and allow you to grow your career.

Requirements:

  • Industry related experience 2+ years;
  • Experience with Photoshop, Illustrator, Fireworks, InDesign, Adobe XD, Acrobat and Sketch tools;
  • Knowledge of web markup languages (CSS, HTML);
  • Knowledge of web design, user experience strategies, and techniques;
  • Solid understanding in creating cross-browser web-based solutions;
  • Basic knowledge of iOS and Android design guidelines;
  • Creativity and ability to provide solutions;
  • Quick learner;
  • At least intermediate English level.

Benefits and Perks:

  • continuous improvement;
  • 20 days vacation;
  • office in centre IF;
  • participation in conferences and seminars;
  • swimming pool, gym and yoga tickets for free;
  • leadership School and internal tech talks;
  • foreign languages courses.
